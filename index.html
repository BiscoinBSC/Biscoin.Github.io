Decentralized settlement currency

Biscoin is a digital currency that can be settled by everyone in a currency, and can be used to realize a bank that can be settled anywhere in the world.

Everyone can participate

Biscoin plans to establish a settlement node on each of the seven continents in the world, and everyone can apply for settlement center nodes.

Secure and private

Biscoin is based on the CryptoNote implementation, which provides complete anonymous payments, millisecond determinations, undetectable payments, dual spending certifications, and future features.

Business ecosystem

Provides support for any commercial payment application system platform, such as mall payment, personal payment, exchange payment, etc.
